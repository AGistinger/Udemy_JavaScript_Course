Pig Game Instructions

To play the pig game select the "Roll Dice" button. Each time that you roll the dice your current score will update with the total amount you have rolled. However if you roll a "1" you will lose all your points for that round and it will be the next players turn. To keep your current amount of points and pass lead to the next player select the "Hold" button. The first player to get to "100" points wins!
